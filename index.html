Anime and Philosophy

Anime is famous for it's story telling, but did you know about the philosophies hidden in anime?

Some Characters and their Philosophy

Uchiha Obito: A famous character that portrays the philosophy of Nihilism or that life is meaningless

Giyu Tomiyoka: A famous character that portrays the philosophy of Pragmatism and being rational.

Levi Ackermann: Levi is one of the most popular characters from Attack on Titan and is famous because of his character's portrayal of Stoicism.

Thorfinn: Thorfinn's overall philosophy which feels super human to the viewer makes him a great example for Existentialism.

If you don’t like your destiny, don’t accept it. Instead have the courage to change it the way you want it to be.
